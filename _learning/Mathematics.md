<h3><strong>Department Vision</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Confident Maths Learners<br>
Competent Maths Teachers<br>
Effective Problem Solvers</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">In line with our school vision, mission and values, we wish to develop in our students the mathematical knowledge and skills that are required in many disciplines and for everyday use as well as to build confidence and foster interest in Mathematics.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">With the belief that every child can learn Mathematics, and through the dedicated support and guidance from our competent Maths teachers, every Lianhuarean will become an effective problem solver.</p>

<img width="150" alt="Mathematics" src="/images/Learning/Mathematics/s3.png">

<p style="text-align: justify;"><strong><i>Learn by Doing</i></strong><br>
Students actively participate in learning experiences through practical activities and the use of manipulatives.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;"><strong><i>Learn by Interacting</i></strong><br>
Students learn from one another by communicating ideas and justifying their thinking to gain deeper understanding.</p>
